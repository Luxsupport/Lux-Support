## 4. Sharing of Information

We do **not** sell your personal data.

### Third-Party Payment Processing (Stripe)

Creators who choose to enable **subscriptions** on Lux are required to sign up with **Stripe**, a third-party payment processor, in order to receive subscription funds. This process is **optional** and only applies to users who choose to monetize their content.

When signing up for Stripe, certain **regulated financial and identity data** may be collected directly by Stripe in accordance with legally required privacy notices under applicable financial services and data protection laws and regulations (including, where applicable, GDPR or GLBA).

Lux does **not** collect or store sensitive financial information such as bank account or payment card details. Stripe processes this data independently under its own privacy policy.

Such data:

* Is collected only for payment processing and compliance purposes
* Is **not shared** with unaffiliated third parties for marketing or advertising
* Is **not linked** with third-party data for advertising purposes
* Is **not sold** to data brokers
* May be shared only for fraud detection, prevention, security, legal compliance, or with consumer reporting agencies where required for credit or identity verification

We may otherwise share limited information only:

* To comply with legal obligations
* To enforce our terms or protect users
* With service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., hosting, analytics)
